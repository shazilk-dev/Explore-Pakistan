Cultural Insights

Festivals & Traditions

Pakistan has lively local festivals and long-standing traditions. Popular events include:

  • Basant (kite festival in Punjab) — colourful, but check local safety rules.
  • Urs — Sufi shrine celebrations with qawwali and community food.
  • Weddings — multi-day events with music, dress and special food.

Must-Try Foods

Try regional favourites — street food and home cooking are highlights:

  • Biryani — aromatic rice and spiced meat (Karachi style popular).
  • Nihari & Paya — slow-cooked breakfast stews.
  • Sweets — jalebi, gulab jamun and barfi for dessert.

Travel Tips

Transport

Intercity buses are affordable and common; domestic flights save time for long distances. Book ahead during busy periods.

Accommodation

Guesthouses and budget hotels are easy to find; big cities also have international chains. Check recent reviews before booking.

Safety & Customs

Dress modestly in conservative areas, avoid political demonstrations, and ask locals for up-to-date advice.

Money & Connectivity

Carry some cash for markets; mobile data is widely available in cities. Keep copies of important documents.
